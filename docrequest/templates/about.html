About Our Document Request System

Streamlining academic document processing for students, alumni, and faculty

Our Mission

To provide an efficient, user-friendly platform for students, alumni, and faculty to submit and track academic document requests online, eliminating the need for physical visits to the registrar's office and reducing processing time. We believe in making academic administrative processes simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone in our academic community.

What We Do

24/7 Availability

Submit document requests anytime, anywhere, at your convenience

Real-Time Tracking

Monitor the status of your requests with live updates

Instant Notifications

Get notified immediately about request updates

Wide Range

Access multiple academic document types in one platform

How It Works

1

Register

Create your account using your school ID and email. Verify your identity to access the system securely.

2

Request

Select the document type, fill in required details, and submit your request. Upload any supporting documents if needed.

3

Track

Monitor your request status from your dashboard. Receive real-time updates via email and system notifications.

4

Collect

Get notified when your document is ready. Visit the registrar's office during business hours to claim your document.

Important Information

Processing Time: Standard processing takes 3-5 business days for most documents. Transcripts and authenticated documents may take 5-7 business days. Rush processing (additional fee) is available for urgent requests. Documents must be claimed at the Registrar's Office during office hours with a valid ID. Please ensure your payment is complete before processing begins.
